Meet the Students

These 10 students represent the demographics of our survey (73% female, 27% male). Let's follow them through a typical day of eating.

Breakfast

Breakfast is often skipped or rushed. While some meet the USDA standards, watch how the gap begins for our 10 students.

Lunch

Between classes, convenience takes over. Notice how refined grains and low fruit intake start to show.

Snack

The afternoon slump brings snack-heavy, lower-quality choices for many, while a few opt for plant-forward options.

Dinner

The final meal of the day. A chance to catch up on nutrition, but irregularity and low dairy/fish consumption persist.
